### AI Model and Tool Releases
- **Anthropic's Claude Opus 4.6**: Anthropic launched an upgraded model with multi-agent teams, a one-million token context window in beta, and enhanced capabilities for long-horizon tasks like document analysis, spreadsheets, and financial workflows. This release emphasizes enterprise safety and pushes into knowledge work beyond coding.
- **Anthropic's Cowork Expansion**: Added customizable plug-ins to Cowork, allowing enterprises to automate workflows in marketing, legal, and support. Several internal plug-ins were open-sourced, responding to demand for configurable AI agents.
- **OpenAI's Frontier Platform**: OpenAI introduced Frontier, a service for enterprises to build and manage AI agents integrated with third-party systems, accelerating adoption and competing directly with Anthropic in workflow automation.
- **OpenAI's Outcome Contracts**: A new feature binding autonomous agents to measurable business results, shifting from task-based to outcome-driven AI.
- **Google's Enterprise Agent Hubs**: Unveiled coordination hubs that dynamically assign work based on AI agent expertise and capacity, enhancing multi-agent collaboration.
- **Toyota's Self-Learning Assembly AI**: Deployed AI that redesigns workflows in real-time to minimize downtime, advancing autonomous manufacturing.
